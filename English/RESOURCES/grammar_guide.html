📌 What is Word Form?

TOEIC Part 5 cho 4 dạng của cùng 1 từ gốc. Bạn phải xác định loại từ nào phù hợp với vị trí trong câu.

📋 4 Loại Từ & Vị Trí

Loại từVị trí trong câuDấu hiệu nhận biết
NOUN (danh từ)Subject, ObjectSau: the / a / an / his / her / our / any / some / every + ___
VERB (động từ)Sau subjectSau modal: can/will/must/should/may + ___
ADJECTIVE (tính từ)Trước noun, sau be/seem___ + noun hoặc be/seem/look + ___
ADVERB (trạng từ)Bổ nghĩa verb/adjThường kết thúc -ly, đứng trước adj hoặc sau verb

🔑 Công thức nhận biết nhanh

the + ___ + of → cần NOUN → "the implementation of the plan"
very / quite / extremely + ___ → cần ADJECTIVE → "very efficient"
___ + noun (không có "the") → thường là ADJECTIVE → "an important meeting"
verb + ___ (cuối câu) → cần ADVERB → "works efficiently"

⚠️ TOEIC Traps

Đừng chọn từ nghe "có vẻ đúng" — hãy xác định VỊ TRÍ trước.
"The financially report" ❌ → cần ADJECTIVE: "The financial report" ✅
"We need to efficiency" ❌ → cần VERB: "We need to work efficiently" ✅

📌 Các Thì Quan Trọng Trong TOEIC

ThìCấu trúcDấu hiệuVí dụ TOEIC
Simple PastV-ed / V2yesterday, last week, in 2020, agoThe meeting was held last Monday.
Simple PresentV / V-severy day, usually, alwaysShe submits reports every Friday.
Present Perfecthas/have + V3since, for, already, just, yet, recentlyThe team has completed the project.
Past Perfecthad + V3by the time, before, after, when (quá khứ)By the time he arrived, we had finished.
Futurewill + V / be going totomorrow, next week, soonThe report will be submitted tomorrow.

🔑 Past Perfect — Quy tắc quan trọng

Khi câu có "by the time + past", mệnh đề kia dùng Past Perfect.

By the time the client arrived, the team had finished the presentation.
Before she left, she had sent all the files.
By the time he arrived, the meeting finished ❌ → had finished ✅

🔑 Present Perfect vs Simple Past

Present PerfectSimple Past
has/have + V3V-ed / V2
Không có thời gian cụ thểCó thời gian cụ thể (yesterday, in 2020...)
Kết quả còn ảnh hưởng đến hiện tạiSự kiện hoàn toàn trong quá khứ
She has worked here for 5 years. (vẫn đang làm)
She worked here last year. (đã nghỉ rồi)

📌 Bị Động — Passive Voice

Dùng khi chủ thể nhận hành động hoặc khi không biết / không cần nêu người thực hiện.

ThìCấu trúc bị độngVí dụ
Simple Presentam/is/are + V3Reports are submitted weekly.
Simple Pastwas/were + V3The policy was approved last Monday.
Present Perfecthas/have been + V3The contract has been signed.
Futurewill be + V3The results will be announced soon.
Modalmodal + be + V3The form must be submitted by Friday.

🔑 Nhận biết nhanh trong Part 5

Hỏi: "Chủ ngữ TỰ làm việc đó hay được người khác làm?"

The invoice must be paid within 30 days. → Hóa đơn không tự trả → bị động ✅
The manager approved the request. → Manager chủ động phê duyệt → chủ động ✅
The new policy approved by the board ❌ → was approved ✅

⚡ Các cụm hay gặp trong TOEIC

  • is/are required to — được/bị yêu cầu phải
  • is/are expected to — được kỳ vọng
  • is/are scheduled to — được lên lịch
  • is/are advised to — được khuyên
  • will be held — sẽ được tổ chức
  • has been updated — đã được cập nhật

📌 Liên từ quan trọng trong TOEIC

Liên từNghĩaTheo sau

Despite / In spite ofMặc dù+ Noun / V-ing (KHÔNG có S+V)
Because / Since / AsBởi vì+ S + V
Because of / Due to / Owing toBởi vì+ Noun / V-ing
HoweverTuy nhiênĐứng đầu câu + dấu phẩy (không nối 2 mệnh đề)

🔑 Although vs Despite

Although the weather was bad, the conference was held. ✅ (S+V sau although)
Despite the bad weather, the conference was held. ✅ (noun phrase sau despite)
Despite the weather was bad ❌ → Despite không đi với S+V
Although the bad weather ❌ → Although cần mệnh đề đầy đủ

🔑 Because vs Because of

Because the flight was delayed, he missed the meeting. ✅
Because of the flight delay, he missed the meeting. ✅
Because of the flight was delayed ❌

Đại từ quan hệDùng choVí dụ

🔑 Bị động trong mệnh đề quan hệ

The candidate who was interviewed yesterday has 10 years of experience.
The proposal which was submitted last week needs revision.
Nếu danh từ đứng trước là người → who. Nếu là vật → which/that.

⚡ Rút gọn mệnh đề quan hệ

TOEIC Part 6 hay dùng dạng rút gọn:

The report submitted last week = The report which was submitted last week
The manager responsible for the project = The manager who is responsible...

📌 Chủ ngữ - Động từ phải tương đồng

Chủ ngữĐộng từVí dụ
Số ítV-s / V-esThe manager reviews reports daily.
Số nhiềuV (không s)The managers review reports daily.
Each / Every + NV-s (số ít)Each employee is required to sign.
A number of + N (pl)V (số nhiều)A number of employees were absent.
The number of + NV-s (số ít)The number of complaints has decreased.

⚠️ TOEIC Traps

Subject bị tách bởi prepositional phrase: "The quality of the products are" ❌ → is ✅ (subject = quality)
"Each of the employees are" ❌ → is ✅ (each = số ít)
"A number of issues is" ❌ → are ✅ (= some issues → số nhiều)

📌 Động từ + V-ing (Gerund)

Các động từ sau luôn đi với V-ing:

  • suggest — I suggest reviewing the contract.
  • recommend — She recommends hiring more staff.
  • consider — We are considering expanding the office.
  • avoid — Please avoid using personal email.
  • finish — She finished writing the report.
  • enjoy — He enjoys attending conferences.
  • keep — Keep trying.
  • mind — Do you mind waiting?

📌 Động từ + to V (Infinitive)

  • want — She wants to attend the conference.
  • decide — They decided to postpone the meeting.
  • plan — We plan to launch in Q3.
  • agree — He agreed to sign the contract.
  • need — I need to submit the report.
  • expect — We expect to complete by Friday.
  • offer — She offered to help with the project.

⚠️ Common TOEIC Traps

suggest + to review ❌ → suggest + reviewing ✅
avoid + to use ❌ → avoid + using ✅
Preposition (in/on/at/for/of/by) + V → luôn dùng V-ing: "responsible for managing"
