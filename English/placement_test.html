Section A — Vocabulary (5 questions)
Question 1
The project was delayed because of unexpected ________ in the supply chain.
A
disruptions
B
disrupted
C
disruptive
D
disruptively
✅ Correct: A — disruptions
Cần danh từ số nhiều sau "unexpected". "Disruptions" (sự gián đoạn) là danh từ phù hợp.
🔑 TOEIC trap: Đây là dạng câu hỏi Word Form — nhận biết vị trí cần danh từ / tính từ / trạng từ.
Question 2
The manager asked all staff to ________ the new safety procedures immediately.
A
comply with
B
comply to
C
complying with
D
compliance with
✅ Correct: A — comply with
"comply with" là collocation cố định (tuân theo). Sau "to" dùng verb gốc (infinitive).
🔑 TOEIC trap: Đừng nhầm "comply to" — giới từ đi kèm "comply" luôn là "with".
Question 3
Choose the word closest in meaning to "reimburse":
A
punish
B
repay
C
request
D
refuse
✅ Correct: B — repay
"Reimburse" = hoàn tiền / bồi hoàn. Đây là từ vựng tài chính rất phổ biến trong TOEIC.
🔑 Ví dụ: "Employees will be reimbursed for travel expenses."
Question 4
Our company's annual ________ exceeded $5 million last year.
A
revenue
B
revise
C
renewal
D
respond
✅ Correct: A — revenue
"Revenue" = doanh thu. Cần danh từ sau "annual". Các đáp án khác là động từ hoặc sai nghĩa.
🔑 TOEIC Finance: revenue / profit / income / expense / budget
Question 5
The invoice must be ________ within 30 days of receipt.
A
paid
B
paying
C
pay
D
to pay
✅ Correct: A — paid
Cấu trúc bị động: "must be + past participle". Invoice không tự trả tiền → bị động.
🔑 TOEIC trap: "must be paying" sai — không dùng continuous trong bị động ở đây.
Section B — Grammar (5 questions)
Question 6
By the time the client arrived, the team ________ the presentation.
A
finished
B
had finished
C
has finished
D
finish
✅ Correct: B — had finished
"By the time + past simple" → mệnh đề kia dùng Past Perfect (had + V3).
🔑 Dấu hiệu: "By the time..." = hành động hoàn thành TRƯỚC một mốc thời gian quá khứ.
Question 7
The new policy ________ by the board of directors last Monday.
A
approved
B
was approved
C
has approved
D
approves
✅ Correct: B — was approved
"Policy" không tự phê duyệt → bị động. "last Monday" → quá khứ đơn bị động.
🔑 Cấu trúc: was/were + past participle.
Question 8
________ the weather was bad, the conference was held as scheduled.
A
Despite
B
Although
C
However
D
Because
✅ Correct: B — Although
Cần conjunction (liên từ) nối 2 mệnh đề đầy đủ. "Although + S + V". "Despite + noun/V-ing".
🔑 TOEIC trap: "Despite the bad weather" ✅ nhưng "Despite the weather was bad" ❌.
Question 9
The candidate ________ interviewed yesterday has 10 years of experience.
A
who was
B
which was
C
who is
D
whose
✅ Correct: A — who was
"Candidate" = người → dùng "who". Bị động (interviewed) + thời quá khứ → "who was".
🔑 who = người, which = vật, whose = sở hữu.
Question 10
I suggest ________ the report before submitting it to the director.
A
review
B
reviewed
C
reviewing
D
to reviewing
✅ Correct: C — reviewing
Các động từ: suggest, recommend, consider, enjoy, avoid, finish → theo sau bằng V-ing.
🔑 suggest + V-ing (không dùng to-infinitive sau suggest).
Section C — Reading Comprehension (3 questions)
📄 MEMO To: All Staff    From: HR Department    Subject: Annual Performance Reviews

Please be advised that annual performance reviews will be conducted from July 14 to July 25. All employees are required to complete the self-assessment form available on the company intranet by July 10. Managers will schedule individual meetings with their team members during the review period. Results will be communicated to employees by August 1.
Question 11
What is the purpose of this memo?
A
To announce a new company policy
B
To inform employees about performance reviews
C
To remind staff about a meeting
D
To introduce a new HR manager
✅ Correct: B
Subject của memo là "Annual Performance Reviews". Nội dung thông báo lịch và quy trình review.
🔑 Reading Part 7: Luôn đọc Subject/Title trước để xác định chủ đề chính.
Question 12
What must employees do before July 10?
A
Meet with their managers
B
Submit their work reports
C
Complete the self-assessment form
D
Attend a training session
✅ Correct: C
Bài đọc nói rõ: "complete the self-assessment form... by July 10".
🔑 Các từ khóa thời gian (by, before, until) thường xuất hiện trong câu hỏi TOEIC Part 7.
Question 13
When will employees receive their review results?
A
July 10
B
July 14
C
July 25
D
August 1
✅ Correct: D — August 1
"Results will be communicated to employees by August 1."
🔑 TOEIC trap: Có nhiều ngày trong bài → đọc kỹ từng ngày gắn với sự kiện nào.
Section D — Listening Simulation (2 questions)
🎧 Short Conversation (written format)

Man: Excuse me, I'd like to return this jacket. I bought it last week but the zipper is broken.
Woman: I'm sorry to hear that. Do you have your receipt?
Man: Yes, here it is.
Woman: No problem. Would you like a refund or an exchange?
Question 14
Why does the man want to return the jacket?
A
It doesn't fit.
B
The color is wrong.
C
The zipper is broken.
D
He changed his mind.
✅ Correct: C
Man nói: "the zipper is broken". Đây là lý do trực tiếp.
🔑 Listening Part 3: Luôn đọc câu hỏi TRƯỚC khi nghe để biết cần nghe gì.
Question 15
What does the woman offer the man?
A
A discount on a new jacket
B
A refund or an exchange
C
A repair service
D
A store credit only
✅ Correct: B
Woman hỏi: "Would you like a refund or an exchange?" — đưa ra 2 lựa chọn.
🔑 TOEIC trap: "store credit only" sai vì woman đưa ra 2 lựa chọn, không phải 1.
