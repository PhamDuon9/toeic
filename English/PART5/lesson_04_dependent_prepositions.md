# PART 5 — LESSON 04: DEPENDENT PREPOSITIONS

**Skill:** Grammar · **Level:** Intermediate · **Date:** 2026-06-24
**TOEIC Part:** Part 5, Part 6

Prepositions that **always follow** certain nouns, verbs, and adjectives. Very common in TOEIC Part 5.

---

## 1. NOUNS + PREPOSITIONS

### + of
advantage, awareness, cause, combination, danger, effect, example, idea, indication, kind, knowledge, opinion, part, possibility, price, probability, process, quality, result, source, supply, understanding, use, variety

### + with
appointment, agreement, contact, connection, contract, dealings, involvement, meeting, negotiations, problem, satisfaction

### + in
belief, change, decrease, experience, improvement, increase, interest, participation, rise

### + for
cure, demand, excuse, explanation, idea, need, reason, reputation, responsibility, time

### + to
approach, contribution, exception, solution

### + on
effect, impact, influence, reliance

### + about
information, truth

---

## 2. ADJECTIVES / PARTICIPLES + PREPOSITIONS

| Preposition | Adjectives |
|-------------|-----------|
| **+ of** | afraid, aware, capable, certain, conscious, frightened, full, ignorant, made, proud, short, sick, sure, tired, typical |
| **+ by** | amazed, annoyed, astonished, concerned, disappointed, excited, impressed, inspired, shocked, surprised |
| **+ about** | anxious, careless, concerned, confused, enthusiastic, excited, happy, optimistic, sorry |
| **+ with** | acquainted, angry, annoyed, bored, compatible, content, disappointed, impressed, pleased, satisfied, unfamiliar |
| **+ in** | disappointed, involved, interested, proficient, rich, specialized, successful |
| **+ to** | accustomed, attached, essential, identical, inferior, preferable, related, similar, superior |
| **+ at** | amazed, astonished, expert, frustrated, good, surprised, terrible, useless |
| **+ for** | eager, concerned, eligible, famous, necessary, ready, responsible, suitable |
| **+ on** | based, dependent, insistent |

|------|---------|-------|
