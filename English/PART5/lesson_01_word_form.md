# PART 5 — LESSON 01: WORD FORM

**Skill:** Grammar · **Level:** Beginner · **Date:** 2026-06-23

## Core Strategy

TOEIC Part 5 Word Form questions give you 4 forms of the same word.
You must identify WHICH form fits the sentence.

### The 4 Positions
| Position | Form Needed | Signals |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| Subject / Object | NOUN | after the/a/an, after adjective |
| After be/seem/become | ADJECTIVE | describes a noun |
| Modifies verb/adj | ADVERB | usually ends -ly |
| Action in sentence | VERB | after modal/to/subject |

### Word Family Example
| Noun | Verb | Adjective | Adverb |
|------|------|-----------|--------|
| disruption | disrupt | disruptive | disruptively |
| approval | approve | approved | — |
| payment | pay | payable | — |
| efficiency | — | efficient | efficiently |
