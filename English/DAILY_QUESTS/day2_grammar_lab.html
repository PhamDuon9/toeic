⏱ Verb Forms and Tenses

Present Time

TenseUse forExample
Present Simpleroutines · habits · always trueIan never opens his mail until lunchtime.
Present Continuous
am/is/are + -ing		happening now · around nowNaomi is giving a presentation in Room 5.
Present Perfect
have/has + p.p.		began in past → continues now · general experience · very recent eventsDaryl has lived in New York for three years.
Present Perfect Continuous
have/has been + -ing		longer past actions → continue now · past action has present resultI'm tired because I have been playing tennis.

Past Time

TenseUse forExample
Past Simplecompleted actions · consecutive actions · past habitsI left DX International two months ago.
Past Continuous
was/were + -ing		longer actions in past · interrupted actionsSteve was studying hard all weekend.
Past Perfect
had + p.p.		action before another past actionWhen we arrived, the meeting had already started.

Future Time

FormUse forExample
Present Simplescheduled events · time clauses (when/if/before/as soon as)When Sharon arrives, please call me.
Present ContinuousarrangementsWe're meeting Mr. Watanabe at 3 o'clock.
going toplans · intentions · predictionsWhen are you going to buy a copy machine?
will / will be + -ingpredictions · future actions in progressSales will improve steadily over the summer.
will have + p.p.complete before a future timeI will have finished this report by Friday.
⚠️ TOEIC TRAP — Time Clauses
✅ When she arrives, I will call her.
❌ When she will arrive, I will call her.
Sau when/if/as soon as/before/after → dùng Present Simple, KHÔNG dùng will.

Suffix Reference

⚡ VERB suffixes

actions, feelings, events

-en-ize-ify-ate

strengthen, prioritize, intensify, aggravate

📦 NOUN suffixes

people, objects, places

-tion-sion-ance-ence-ment-ness-ity-ship-or-ist

reduction, independence, accomplishment, membership

🎨 ADJECTIVE suffixes

describe nouns

-ful-able-ible-worthy-al-ical-ous-ish-ic-ary

resourceful, understandable, tactical, foolish

🏃 ADVERB suffixes

describe verbs or adjectives

-ly-ally

hurriedly, faintly, enthusiastically

⚠️ hard, fast — không có -ly

⚠️ TOEIC TRAP — Adverb "hard" vs "hardly"
✅ All the apprentices tried hard to do well. (hard = adverb: cố gắng)
❌ All the apprentices tried hardly... (hardly = hầu như không — nghĩa ngược)
