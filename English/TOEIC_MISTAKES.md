## Placement Test Mistakes — 2026-06-23

### Q1 — Vocabulary / Word Form
- **Question:** "unexpected ________ in the supply chain"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect)
- **Correct:** A — disruptions (noun plural)
- **Error type:** Word Form — did not identify that a noun was needed after "unexpected"
- **Rule:** adjective + _noun_ (unexpected disruptions). Learn to identify noun slots.

### Q2 — Vocabulary / Collocation
- **Question:** "staff to ________ the new safety procedures"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect)
- **Correct:** A — comply with
- **Error type:** Fixed collocation — "comply WITH" not "comply TO"
- **Rule:** Memorize verb + preposition pairs: comply with, result in, apply for, refer to

### Q3 — Vocabulary / Synonym
- **Question:** Meaning of "reimburse"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect)
- **Correct:** B — repay
- **Error type:** Business vocabulary gap
- **Rule:** reimburse = hoàn tiền/bồi hoàn. High-frequency TOEIC finance word.

### Q4 — Vocabulary / Word Form
- **Question:** "annual ________ exceeded $5 million"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect)
- **Correct:** A — revenue
- **Error type:** Business vocabulary gap + Word Form (need noun after "annual")
- **Rule:** revenue = doanh thu. Learn: revenue / profit / expense / budget / invoice

### Q5 — Vocabulary / Passive Voice
- **Question:** "The invoice must be ________"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect)
- **Correct:** A — paid
- **Error type:** Passive voice structure: must be + past participle
- **Rule:** be + V3 = passive. "must be paid" = hóa đơn phải được thanh toán.

### Q6 — Grammar / Past Perfect
- **Question:** "By the time the client arrived, the team ________ the presentation"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect — selected wrong tense)
- **Correct:** B — had finished
- **Error type:** Tense sequence — Past Perfect not identified
- **Rule:** "By the time + past simple" → Past Perfect (had + V3) in the other clause.

### Q7 — Grammar / Passive Voice
- **Question:** "The new policy ________ by the board last Monday"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect)
- **Correct:** B — was approved
- **Error type:** Passive voice in past simple
- **Rule:** Subject receives action → passive. "last Monday" → past → was/were + V3.

### Q8 — Grammar / Conjunction
- **Question:** "________ the weather was bad, the conference was held"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect)
- **Correct:** B — Although
- **Error type:** Conjunction vs. preposition confusion
- **Rule:** Although + S + V (full clause). Despite + noun/V-ing (no full clause).

### Q9 — Grammar / Relative Clause
- **Question:** "The candidate ________ interviewed yesterday"
- **Your answer:** ❌ (incorrect)
- **Correct:** A — who was
- **Error type:** Relative pronoun selection + passive in relative clause
- **Rule:** Person = who. Passive in clause = who was + V3.
